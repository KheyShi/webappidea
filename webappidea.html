About

DencityTraQR is a web application that monitors the population density of each place and establishment in Davao city with the use of the data from contact tracing providers and with digital maps. This is to ensure that customers can have the necessary information before going outside and for business owners to create plans to effectively control the visitors in their shops. Aside from that, it provides a convenient way to store multiple QR codes for various areas through the My QR feature.

Our application may have similarities to existing ones. These include SafeDavaoQR, DOLE QR for COVID-19, and Waitz by UC San Diego. One of the main differences between the three is that DencityTraQR relies on contact tracing providers such as SafeDavaoQR as data providers and does not employ physical sensors, effectively lowering the cost for maintenance.

The target market for our web application is for the people living in Davao city who are business owners and customers who frequently go outside for various needs. As of 2020, the city currently has 1.8 million residents. Furthermore, we specifically want to help almost 40% of Davaoenos who are part of the dependent population as they are the ones who are most vulnerable during these times.

The business model of DencityTraQR is to rely on the revenues generated on advertisements and donations from kind individuals who see the value of these kinds of websites. In the future, we will plan to provide paid subscription service for businesses that provide an additional set of tools to manage their shops.

Our way of going to shops and conducting businesses will never be the same as before. But with DencityTraQR, we can slowly go back to the joy of pre-pandemic days in a safe manner.
